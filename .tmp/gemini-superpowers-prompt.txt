Product name: handsfree-pdf-reader
Concept: A browser-based hands-free PDF reader that uses webcam-based wink detection and head/nose direction to turn pages or scroll, positioned as a browser-first alternative to foot pedals and app-only solutions.

Target users:
- Musicians reading sheet music
- Heavy PDF readers (researchers, students, professionals)
- Presenters
- Accessibility users needing hands-free navigation

Differentiators:
- Browser-first
- Local-first privacy (camera processing on-device/in-browser)
- Fast calibration for per-user personalization
- Document-specific modes (sheet music, paper reading, presentation)
